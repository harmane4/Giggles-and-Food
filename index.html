Giggles and Food

Keep abreast with information about restaurants with play equipments around the Perth area. Our team of developers predicted an increase in the demand on such restaurants once the 2020 pandemic reached phase four. Choose restaurants that have play equipment. Watch children play, eat and relax while you wait for your meal or are simply enjoying time with your loved ones. Our application let's you choose these restaurants view ratings, give ratings and help plan your trip. Let us reconnect back to people and nature.

About Us

Hi! We are three young Web Developers( A mum, School Teacher and an Event Manager) who have made this app just for you. The Giggles and Food application has been made keeping families, guardians and care takers, of young children in mind. We want family outings,catch ups, play dates to be extra fun. We would love to hear from you guys about what you think and what you would like to see. We will keep updating our application with demands made by you.Give us a shout! Follow us at@giggles.food or score awsome freebies (who does not love a freebie?) by joining our email list. Help us help you to look at all your options at the click of your finger.
